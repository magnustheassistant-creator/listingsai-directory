The 5 Funnels Every Real Estate Agent Should Have

Here are the highest-impact funnels real estate agents typically build first. You don't need all of them to start — even one working funnel can transform your business.

1. Buyer Lead Magnet Funnel

This is the most common starting point. You offer something valuable — a neighborhood guide, a first-time buyer's checklist, a mortgage calculator — in exchange for the prospect's email address and basic contact info.

The funnel works like this:

  • Landing page with a compelling offer
  • Form asking for name, email, and a qualifying question (e.g., "What's your estimated budget?")
  • Thank-you page with the free download + a brief pitch
  • Email sequence that delivers the content and introduces your services

2. Property Valuation Funnel

Offer a free home valuation in exchange for contact info. This attracts sellers who are thinking about listing — one of the highest-value segments in real estate.

The key here is the qualifying question: ask for their address, approximate timeline, and reason for selling. This gives you enough to prioritize follow-up intelligently.

3. Open House Registration Funnel

Driving foot traffic to an open house? Build a simple funnel that collects RSVPs and follow-up information. The data you collect is gold — these are people who are actively looking in that neighborhood right now.

4. FSBO (For Sale By Owner) Lead Funnel

FSBO leads are motivated sellers who tried to do it themselves and are now realizing they need help. Offer a "FSBO escape plan" or free consultation. These leads convert at higher rates than cold leads because they're already in the process of selling.

5. Investor Lead Funnel

Real estate investors move fast and close fast. A funnel offering off-market deal alerts or a market analysis report can attract cash buyers who want to close within 30 days.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a buyer persona and why do I need one?

A buyer persona is a fictional profile of your ideal client. It helps you understand their goals, frustrations, and decision-making process so you can create marketing that resonates and messages that convert.

How many personas should I create?

Most real estate agents benefit from 3-5 distinct personas covering different life stages: first-time buyers, upgrade buyers, downsizing empty nesters, and investors. You can always add more as your business evolves.

Can I use this for both buyers and sellers?

Absolutely. The same personas work for listing presentations too. Understanding what your sellers want (quick sale, max price, stress-free process) helps you position your value proposition correctly.

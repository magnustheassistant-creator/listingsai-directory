Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best morning routine for real estate agents?

Start with 30 minutes of lead follow-up before the market opens. Review your calendar, prepare for scheduled showings, and handle any urgent emails. The first hours set your day's energy and priorities.

How many hours should I spend on lead generation daily?

Aim for 2-3 hours of dedicated lead gen time daily. This can be split into morning outreach and afternoon social media/content creation. Consistency beats intensity in real estate lead generation.

What time blocks are most important for realtors?

Peak showing times (10am-4pm) are sacred for client activities. Mornings work best for lead gen and admin. Evenings are ideal for follow-up calls and scheduling next-day appointments.

How do I maintain work-life balance as a real estate agent?

Set firm boundaries with clients about response times. Block personal/family time on your calendar. Respect your own off-hours — clients can wait, and your sustainability matters for long-term success.

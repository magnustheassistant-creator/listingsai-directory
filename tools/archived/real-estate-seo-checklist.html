Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to see SEO results for real estate websites?

Most real estate agents see initial improvements in 3-6 months with consistent effort. Technical fixes can show results faster within 2-4 weeks. Building domain authority through content and backlinks typically takes 6-12 months.

What's the most important SEO factor for realtors?

Local SEO is crucial for real estate agents. Google Business Profile optimization, local citations, and location-based content typically drive the most qualified leads for realtors.

Do I need to hire an SEO expert for my real estate website?

You can handle basic SEO yourself using this checklist and tools like Surfer SEO. For comprehensive technical SEO, schema markup, and advanced content strategy, working with an SEO professional can accelerate your results.

How often should I update my real estate website SEO?

Review your SEO quarterly and update content regularly. Add new blog posts 2-4 times monthly, update listing pages as inventory changes, and refresh market statistics monthly to stay relevant.

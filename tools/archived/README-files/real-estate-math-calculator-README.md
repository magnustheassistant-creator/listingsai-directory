## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: How do you calculate mortgage payments??
**Monthly payment uses the standard amortization formula. This tool handles it instantly. For a $400K loan at 7% on a 30-year fixed, the payment is approximately $2,661 per month (principal and interest).**

### Q: How do you calculate real estate agent commission??
**Commission = Sale Price x Commission Rate. For a $500,000 sale at 6% commission = $30,000 total. Agent take-home depends on their split: at 70% split = $21,000, at 80% split = $24,000.**

### Q: What math do real estate agents use most??
**The most-used calculations: mortgage payment, commission, seller net proceeds, buyer total costs (PITI plus closing costs), and investment metrics (cap rate, cash-on-cash return).**

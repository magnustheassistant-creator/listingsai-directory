## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What is a sphere of influence in real estate??
**Your sphere of influence (SOI) is everyone you know who might refer you business or become a client. This includes past clients, family, friends, coworkers, neighbors, doctors, lawyers, accountants, financial advisors, and anyone else in your network. Top agents systematically nurture their SOI to generate consistent referrals.**

### Q: How many people should be in your real estate SOI??
**Aim for 300-500 quality contacts minimum. Many top agents have 1,000+ contacts in their SOI. Quality matters more than quantity - a well-nurtured database of 300 actively engaged contacts will generate more business than 1,000 names you never touch.**

### Q: How often should you contact your SOI??
**Minimum monthly touchpoints for your top 20% of contacts. Quarterly touchpoints for your full database. The key is consistency - your SOI should hear from you on a predictable schedule. Missing touchpoints means missed referrals.**

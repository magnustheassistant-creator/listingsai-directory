## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What are the most common real estate objections??
**The top objections are: "It's overpriced" (and the related "I can get it cheaper"), "We're not ready yet," "The market is going to crash," "We're already working with an agent," and "We're going to wait for a better time." Each has specific response strategies.**

### Q: How do you respond to "The market is going to crash"??
**Acknowledge the concern, then provide data. "I understand the concern — let's look at what's actually happened in our market over the last 5 years. Historically, [market name] has averaged X% annual appreciation. Even in downturns, real estate locally has [specific data]. What I focus on is whether this specific property meets your needs and budget."**

### Q: What's the best objection handling formula??
**The Feel-Felt-Found formula works well: "I understand how you feel. Other clients have felt the same way. What they found was..." Then provide a specific example that addresses their concern. It validates their concern, creates social proof, and provides a solution.**

3. Practice your responses out loud before you need them — the first time you respond to an objection should not be live on a call

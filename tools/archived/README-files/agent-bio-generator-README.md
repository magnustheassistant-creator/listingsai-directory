## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What makes a great real estate agent bio??
**A great bio: (1) leads with your unique value proposition - what makes you different, (2) includes specific results and achievements, (3) speaks to your ideal client directly, (4) has a genuine human voice - not corporate, (5) includes a clear call-to-action. Most agent bios are too generic - specificity builds trust.**

### Q: How long should an agent bio be??
**For websites: 150-300 words works well. For social media profiles: 100-150 words for LinkedIn, under 160 characters for Twitter. For speaking or publications: 50-75 words for speaker bios. Always have a long version and short version ready.**

### Q: Should you include personal information in your agent bio??
**Yes - clients hire agents they know, like, and trust. Including a personal element (family, hobbies, community involvement) humanizes you and builds connection. Just keep it professional and relevant. "I have lived in Austin for 15 years and love helping families find their dream homes" is more compelling than just listing credentials.**

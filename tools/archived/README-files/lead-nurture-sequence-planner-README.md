## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: How long should a real estate nurture sequence be??
**For real estate, a 14-30 day sequence works best for most leads. Too short and you have not built enough trust. Too long and leads forget why they signed up. The sequence should provide consistent value over 2-4 weeks before a sales call.**

### Q: How often should you email nurture leads??
**Every 2-3 days is the sweet spot for real estate nurture sequences. Frequent enough to stay top of mind, not so frequent that they unsubscribe. After the initial sequence, drop to monthly touchpoints.**

### Q: What content works best in nurture sequences??
**Value-first content: market updates, neighborhood reports, helpful tips, and educational material. Save the pitch for the end of the sequence. Each email should provide genuine value and move the lead closer to being ready to act.**

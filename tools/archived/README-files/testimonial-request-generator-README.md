## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: When is the best time to ask for a testimonial??
**The ideal time is within 48 hours of closing — the transaction is fresh, the client is happy, and the emotional high leads to enthusiastic responses. If you miss that window, the 30-day mark (when they're settling into the new home) is the second-best time.**

### Q: How do you get clients to leave reviews on specific platforms??
**Make it easy. Include direct links to your Zillow, Google, and Realtor.com review pages in your request. Reduce friction to zero. Also ask face-to-face right after closing — most clients will agree to leave a review if you just ask directly.**

### Q: What makes a testimonial more effective??
**Specificity beats general praise. "She responded to every text within minutes" is more powerful than "She was great." When collecting testimonials, guide clients toward specific results and numbers: "What was the exact price you sold for?" and "What was your favorite part of working with me?"**

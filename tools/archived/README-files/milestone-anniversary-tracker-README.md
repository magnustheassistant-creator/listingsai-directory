## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What are the most valuable milestone touchpoints??
**Home purchase anniversary is the most valuable — it's when clients are most reflective on their decision and most likely to refer. Birthday is second. The annual home anniversary (the day they moved in) should trigger a personal note and market update.**

### Q: How do you remember all your client milestones??
**The only way is to use a tracking system. Add all important dates to your CRM with annual reminders. Review your upcoming milestones every Monday morning. Five minutes of planning prevents missed opportunities.**

### Q: What should you send for a home purchase anniversary??
**A personal note acknowledging the milestone ("One year ago today, you moved into your dream home at [address]. How's it been?") combined with a small thoughtful gesture. If you've done market updates for them, reference how their home's value has changed.**

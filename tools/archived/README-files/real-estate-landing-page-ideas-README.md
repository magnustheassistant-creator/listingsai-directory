## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What makes a real estate landing page convert??
**The most important elements: (1) A single specific headline that matches the ad that brought them there, (2) A compelling offer of genuine value, (3) Minimal form fields, (4) A clear single call-to-action, (5) No navigation links that let them leave.**

### Q: How many form fields should a real estate landing page have??
**As few as possible. Name and email is ideal for most campaigns. Phone number adds friction - only ask for it if you are prepared to call immediately. Additional fields will reduce submission rates by 20-40%.**

### Q: What offer works best for real estate landing pages??
**Free home valuation reports, buyer guides for first-time buyers, neighborhood market reports, and free consultations all convert well. The key is the offer must be genuinely valuable and specific to your market.**

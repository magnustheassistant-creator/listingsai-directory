## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What questions should you ask at an open house sign-in??
**Beyond name and email: current address (neighbor or from far away), timeline (looking now or starting), budget range, current living situation (own/rent), and how they heard about the open house. This data helps qualify leads and personalize follow-up.**

### Q: How do you get more people to sign in at an open house??
**Start conversations before asking for information. Build rapport first. Have the sign-in sheet visible on a nice clipboard or table. Make it feel premium - "Join our exclusive buyer list" works better than "Sign the guest book."**

### Q: What is the best follow-up for open house visitors??
**Text within 2 hours: "Great meeting you at the open house at 123 Oak Street - here is my direct number." The next day, send a market update email about the property. Within a week, follow up with a personalized market report.**

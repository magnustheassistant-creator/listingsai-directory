## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What questions should you ask at an open house sign-in??
**Beyond name and email: current address (are they a neighbor or from far away?), timeline (are they looking now or just starting?), budget range (rough estimate), current living situation (own/rent), and how they found out about the open house. This data helps you qualify leads and personalize follow-up.**

### Q: How do you get more people to sign in at an open house??
**Start the conversation before asking for information. "I'm the agent hosting today — what brought you to the open house?" gets much higher compliance than putting a clipboard in front of people. Also, have the sign-in sheet visible on a nice clipboard or table — don't hide it.**

### Q: What's the best follow-up for open house visitors??
**Send a text within 2 hours ("Great meeting you at the open house at 123 Main St — here's my direct number for any questions"). The next day, send a market update email about the property. Within a week, follow up with a personalized market report. The goal is to start a conversation.**

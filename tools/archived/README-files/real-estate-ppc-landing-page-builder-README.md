## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What is a good quality score for Google Ads real estate??
**Quality Score ranges from 1-10. For real estate, aim for 7 or higher. Quality Score affects your cost-per-click and ad position. Landing page relevance, ad relevance, and expected click-through rate all factor into Quality Score.**

### Q: How do you optimize a real estate PPC landing page??
**Match the landing page headline exactly to the ad headline. Remove all navigation links. Use a single clear call-to-action. Keep form fields minimal (name and email). Include trust signals: testimonials, your photo, recent transactions. Page load speed is critical - 3 seconds or less.**

### Q: How much should real estate agents spend on PPC??
**Depends on market competition. In competitive markets, expect $5-15 per click. A reasonable budget for testing is $500-1000 per month. Track your cost per lead carefully - if it exceeds what a lead is worth to you, PPC may not be profitable.**

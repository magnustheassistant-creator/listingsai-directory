## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What makes a good real estate lead magnet??
**A good lead magnet is specific, valuable, and immediately usable. "The Ultimate First-Time Home Buyer Guide for Austin" converts better than "Home Buying Tips." The more specific to your market and audience, the higher the conversion rate.**

### Q: How do you deliver a lead magnet??
**Most agents use a landing page with an email capture form. Once they submit their email, they get instant access via a PDF download link or a welcome email with the resource. Email automation platforms like ManyChat or Mailchimp handle this easily.**

### Q: Do lead magnets actually generate leads??
**Yes - a well-designed lead magnet can generate 100-500 leads per month for active agents. The key is promoting it consistently through your website, social media, and paid advertising, and following up with every lead within 24 hours.**

## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: When is the best time to ask for referrals??
**The ideal time is 2-3 weeks after closing, when the transaction is complete and the client is settled in their new home. At this point they're happy, the experience is fresh, and asking feels natural. The second-best time is around the one-year anniversary of their purchase.**

### Q: How do you ask for referrals without being awkward??
**Make it easy and natural. "I'm always looking to help people like you — if you know anyone who's thinking about buying or selling, I'd love to be a resource." Don't pressure. Just plant the seed and make it effortless to refer.**

### Q: What's the referral request formula that works??
**The most effective approach: (1) Acknowledge the client by name and reference their specific transaction, (2) State that referrals are a significant part of your business, (3) Make the ask specific and easy, (4) Thank them in advance, (5) Make it effortless to respond.**

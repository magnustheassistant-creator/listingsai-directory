## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: How do real estate agents manage their pipeline??
**Top agents review their pipeline weekly. They track every active deal by stage, note upcoming deadlines, and identify which leads need immediate follow-up. A good pipeline review takes 30 minutes and prevents deals from falling through the cracks.**

### Q: What should be in a real estate pipeline review??
**For each deal: where it is in the process, next critical date, what needs to happen next, and what's blocking progress. For leads: where they are in the buying/selling timeline, when to follow up, and what information they need.**

### Q: How often should you review your pipeline??
**Weekly minimum. Top producers review their pipeline every Monday morning and every Friday afternoon. This keeps deals on track and ensures no lead goes cold.**

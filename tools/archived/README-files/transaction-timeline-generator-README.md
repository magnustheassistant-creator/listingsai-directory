## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What are the critical timeline milestones??
**Key milestones: Contract acceptance and deposit, Home inspection (5-10 days), Financing contingency deadline (21-30 days), Appraisal, Final walkthrough (24-48 hours before closing), Closing.**

### Q: How do you keep a transaction on track??
**Use a systematic tracking tool. Set reminders 3-5 days before every deadline. Follow up with the other agent, lender, and title company weekly.**

### Q: What causes transactions to fall apart??
**Financing issues are the number one cause: buyers getting denied after pre-approval, appraisal issues, and inspection items that cannot be resolved.**

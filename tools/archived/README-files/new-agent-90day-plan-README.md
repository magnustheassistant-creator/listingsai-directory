## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What should a new agent focus on in their first 90 days??
**Priority order: (1) Learn the transaction process inside and out, (2) Build your database of 100+ contacts, (3) Generate leads every single day, (4) Shadow experienced agents on listings and closings, (5) Get your first listing appointment and learn from the experience.**

### Q: How many leads should a new agent generate in 90 days??
**Aim for 50-100 leads in your first 90 days. Quality matters more than quantity - focus on people who are actively buying or selling. Track your conversion rate: how many leads become showings, how many showings become offers.**

### Q: When should a new agent expect their first closing??
**With focused effort, new agents can close their first deal in 60-90 days. It depends on lead quality, market conditions, and how much time they invest in learning and prospecting.**

## About This Tool
The Real Estate Market Update Generator helps agents create professional monthly market reports that keep them top-of-mind with past clients and attract new leads. Consistent market updates are one of the most effective SOI (Sphere of Influence) marketing strategies — they demonstrate expertise, keep you visible, and lead to referrals and repeat business.

This free tool generates complete market update reports including inventory levels, days on market trends, price movement, months of supply, and local market highlights. Each report is formatted professionally and can be sent as an email, printed as a PDF, or posted on social media.

The key to effective SOI marketing is consistency — use this tool every month to never miss a market update.

## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: How often should agents send market updates??
**Monthly is the sweet spot for most agents. Quarterly is fine if you have a smaller database. The key is consistency — your database should expect your update on a specific schedule. Miss one month and people forget you exist.**

### Q: Do market updates actually generate leads??
**Yes — market updates consistently generate referrals and repeat business because they keep you top-of-mind. Studies show agents who stay in touch with past clients get 2-3x more referrals than those who don't.**

### Q: What metrics should a market update include??
**Key metrics: median sales price (YoY and MoM), active inventory, months of supply, average days on market, sale-to-list price ratio, and new pending sales. Include neighborhood-specific data when possible for more relevance.**

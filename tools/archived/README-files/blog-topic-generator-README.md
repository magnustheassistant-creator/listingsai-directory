## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: How often should real estate agents blog??
**Most successful real estate agents blog 2-4 times per month. Consistency matters more than frequency. A monthly blog that ranks well is more valuable than weekly posts that nobody finds.**

### Q: Do real estate blogs actually generate leads??
**Yes — a well-optimized real estate blog can generate 2-5x more organic leads than your website's service pages because it targets informational search queries from people in the early research phase.**

### Q: What makes a real estate blog post rank on Google??
**SEO-optimized titles with target keywords, headers (H2/H3) with related keywords, 800-1500 words of original content, internal links to your service pages, and images with alt text. The blog topic itself must match what people are searching for.**

## Success Tips
1. Choose topics where you already have expertise — your past client questions are your best blog ideas
2. Target long-tail keywords (e.g., "first-time home buyer mistakes in Austin") rather than competitive generic terms
3. Update and republish old blog posts annually — refreshed content outperforms brand new posts in Google rankings
4. Link every blog post to your contact page or a relevant landing page to capture leads

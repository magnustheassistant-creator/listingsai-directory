## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What makes a property video effective??
**The most effective property videos: (1) start with a hook that creates curiosity, (2) focus on the best features, not every room, (3) use natural light when possible, (4) tell a story about the lifestyle, (5) have a clear call-to-action at the end. Keep it short.**

### Q: How do you script a property video??
**Structure: Opening hook (5 seconds), Front porch exterior (5 seconds), Living areas (15-20 seconds), Kitchen highlight (10-15 seconds), Bedroom/bath highlight (10-15 seconds), Outdoor space (10 seconds), Closing call-to-action (5 seconds).**

### Q: What equipment do you need for property videos??
**Smartphone with 4K capability is sufficient for agents starting out. Use a gimbal or stabilizer for smooth movement. Natural lighting is best. Invest in a lavalier microphone for clear audio in empty rooms.**

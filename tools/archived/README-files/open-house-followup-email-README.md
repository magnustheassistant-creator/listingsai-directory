## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: How quickly should you follow up after an open house??
**Immediately after the open house ends. Text or email within 2 hours while the visit is still fresh. Same-day follow-up gets 3-5x more responses than next-day follow-up. The longer you wait, the less likely they remember you.**

### Q: What should you say in an open house follow-up email??
**Reference the specific property: "I noticed you were looking at the kitchen with the quartz countertops at 123 Oak Street." Then offer value: "Based on what you said you're looking for, I have a few other properties you should see." Finally, make it easy to respond with a specific next step.**

### Q: How do you get email addresses at open houses??
**Start conversations before asking for information. Build rapport first. Then ask naturally: "I'd love to send you details on this property and a few others that might fit what you're looking for — what's the best email to reach you?" Always get the phone number too.**

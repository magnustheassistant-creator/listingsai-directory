## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What makes real estate ad copy effective??
**Effective real estate ads: (1) target a specific audience with specific language, (2) create urgency or exclusivity, (3) include a clear value proposition, (4) have a single focused call-to-action. Generic ads that try to reach everyone connect with no one.**

### Q: How do you write Google ads for real estate??
**Google search ads should target specific keywords (e.g., "first-time home buyer agent Austin"), include the keyword in the headline, highlight your unique selling proposition, and have a clear call-to-action. Character limits are tight: 30 characters per headline, 90 per description.**

### Q: What real estate Facebook ad performs best??
**Buyer lead ads with a free value offer (home valuation, first-time buyer guide, market report) outperform direct "call me" ads significantly. The free offer removes the friction of the first contact.**

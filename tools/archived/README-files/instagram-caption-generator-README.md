## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What makes an Instagram caption effective??
**The first line must stop the scroll - it should create curiosity or make a bold statement. Then provide value or tell a story. End with a clear call-to-action. The best captions make people want to respond.**

### Q: How long should real estate Instagram captions be??
**Listing posts: 100-150 words max. Educational content: 150-250 words. Instagram caps captions at 2,200 characters, but posts over 125 words get cut off in feeds - put the most important content first.**

### Q: How many hashtags should real estate agents use??
**For real estate, use a mix: 3-5 branded hashtags, 5-10 niche hashtags, and 10-15 community hashtags. Use the same core hashtags consistently to build your presence.**

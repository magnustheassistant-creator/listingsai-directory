## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: How do you create a lead magnet that converts??
**The formula for high-converting lead magnets: specific audience + specific problem + specific solution = high conversion. "First-Time Home Buyer Guide for Austin" converts better than "Home Buying Guide." The more specific and valuable, the higher the conversion rate.**

### Q: What lead magnet format converts best??
**Checklists and worksheets convert well for educational content. Reports and guides convert well for high-intent leads. Tools and calculators are the highest converting but require more development. For real estate, market reports and buyer guides tend to perform best.**

### Q: Where should you promote your lead magnet??
**Everywhere: your website homepage, every blog post, every social media profile, every email signature, every listing flyer, and paid ads. The more places you promote it, the more leads you capture.**

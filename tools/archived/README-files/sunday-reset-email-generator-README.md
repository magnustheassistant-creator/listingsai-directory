## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What day should I send real estate marketing emails??
**Tuesday through Thursday mornings have the highest open rates for business emails. However, the Sunday Reset email is different — it lands in inboxes on Sunday evening when people are relaxing and more likely to read. Test both approaches and track your open rates.**

### Q: How do you write an email that doesn't sound salesy??
**Focus on providing value first. Share a market insight, offer a helpful tip, or ask a question rather than pitching your services. The goal is to start a conversation, not close a sale. When you help people without expecting anything in return, they remember you when it's time to buy or sell.**

### Q: How often should agents email their sphere??
**Monthly minimum for your full database. Weekly for your top 20% of contacts (past clients who are active in the market, centers of influence). The goal is consistency — your database should expect to hear from you regularly.**

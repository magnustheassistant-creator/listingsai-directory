## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What makes a YouTube title effective??
**Effective titles: (1) include your target keyword early, (2) create curiosity or promise a specific benefit, (3) use numbers when applicable ("7 Tips..."), (4) stay under 60 characters to avoid truncation, (5) match the thumbnail tone. The best titles promise something specific and deliver exactly what they promise.**

### Q: Should YouTube titles match the video content exactly??
**The title should accurately represent the content — misleading titles get clicks but destroy subscriber trust and watch time. Create titles that accurately describe the value while making it irresistible to click.**

### Q: How important is SEO for YouTube titles??
**Very important. YouTube is the world's second-largest search engine. Titles with target keywords in the first 60 characters rank better in both YouTube search and Google search results. Include your main keyword naturally, not stuffed.**

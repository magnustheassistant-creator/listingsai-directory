## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What type of LinkedIn posts perform best for real estate agents??
**Market update posts with specific local data perform consistently well. Thought leadership posts that challenge conventional wisdom get high engagement. Success stories (with permission) are powerful. The worst performing posts are generic motivational quotes and "Just listed!" announcements without context.**

### Q: How often should real estate agents post on LinkedIn??
**3-5 times per week is the sweet spot for agents building a following. Daily posting can seem spammy. Quality matters more than quantity — one well-researched market update outperforms five generic motivational posts.**

### Q: How do you generate leads from LinkedIn without being salesy??
**Focus on providing value and establishing expertise. Post market data, share insights, comment on industry news, and help other professionals. When someone messages you about a post, that's a warm lead — respond within an hour and offer to help. The leads come from the relationship-building, not the selling.**

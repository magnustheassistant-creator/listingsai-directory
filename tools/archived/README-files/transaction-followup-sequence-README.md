## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: When should you ask past clients for referrals??
**The optimal timing is 2-3 weeks post-close when the transaction is fresh but the client has had time to settle in and experience the outcome. For annual touchpoints, send a personalized "how's the home" email around their purchase anniversary. Avoid asking at closing — it's too transactional, too fast.**

### Q: How do you stay top-of-mind with past clients without being annoying??
**The 5-touch annual system works well: (1) Transaction anniversary personal note, (2) Market update during their purchase anniversary month, (3) Holiday card, (4) Helpful tip email (seasonal maintenance reminder, market report), (5) Annual check-in call. Keep each touch valuable but brief.**

### Q: What's the best metric for tracking referral conversion??
**Track your "sphere conversion rate" — the percentage of your sphere that does business with you again or refers someone to you each year. Top agents achieve 15-20% annual conversion. Track this quarterly and grow it systematically.**

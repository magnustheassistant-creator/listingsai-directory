## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: How often should real estate agents send newsletters??
**Monthly is the sweet spot for agents with active databases. Quarterly is sufficient for agents with less frequent contact needs. The key is consistency — your database should expect your newsletter on a regular schedule.**

### Q: What should a real estate newsletter include??
**Key sections: a brief market update with relevant data, a highlight of a recent transaction or testimonial, a community or neighborhood tip, a seasonal real estate tip, and a clear call-to-action with your contact information.**

### Q: Do newsletters really generate business??
**Yes — newsletters are among the highest-ROI marketing activities for real estate agents. Studies show agents who stay in regular contact with past clients generate significantly more referral business than those who do not.**

## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q: What makes a property announcement effective??
**Effective announcements: (1) lead with the most compelling feature, (2) create urgency, (3) include specific details and pricing, (4) have a clear call-to-action, (5) include high-quality photos or video. The goal is to get recipients to act immediately.**

### Q: Should you announce to your sphere first??
**Yes — your database should be the first to know about new listings. Top agents have a first look strategy where their sphere gets 24-48 hours of exclusive access before the public listing launch.**

### Q: How do you make an announcement stand out??
**Lead with a hook that is specific. "Stunning" and "beautiful" are overused — be specific. Use numbers and create genuine scarcity.**

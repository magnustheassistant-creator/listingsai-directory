Frequently Asked Questions

Why are Sunday emails effective for real estate agents?

Sunday is when people are relaxed, planning their week, and thinking about life decisions. A well-crafted Sunday email reaches them in a reflective mindset, making them more receptive to real estate content and more likely to remember you when they need an agent.

How often should I email my database?

Weekly Sunday emails are ideal for staying top of mind without being annoying. Supplement with monthly market updates and triggered emails based on behavior (visited a listing page, filled out a form, etc.).

What should I include in my Sunday reset email?

Keep it valuable and personal: market insights, helpful tips, a brief personal note, and a soft call-to-action. Don't make it all about selling — provide genuine value first. End with an invitation to connect or reach out.

How do I grow my email list as a real estate agent?

Offer lead magnets like market reports, home buyer guides, or neighborhood guides. Use landing pages, social media, and in-person networking to collect emails. Always get permission and provide clear value for subscribing.

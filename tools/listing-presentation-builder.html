Frequently Asked Questions

What should be included in a listing presentation?

A strong listing presentation includes: your introduction and value proposition, property details and highlights, market analysis and pricing strategy, marketing plan, comparable sold data, your experience and testimonials, and next steps.

How do I price a property competitively?

Use CMA (Comparative Market Analysis) looking at recently sold similar properties, current active listings, and expired listings. Consider price per square foot, days on market trends, and the property's specific features and condition.

What's the best way to present market data?

Use visuals like charts and graphs in your presentation. Show 6-month trends, absorption rate, and compare your market to regional trends. Data-backed pricing recommendations build trust with sellers.

How many slides should a listing presentation have?

Keep it focused with 10-15 slides. Cover the essentials without overwhelming clients. Leave detailed handouts for take-home material. Respect their time while demonstrating your professionalism.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical real estate transaction take?

Most transactions close in 30-45 days from offer acceptance. Cash deals can close faster (7-14 days) while financed purchases typically take 30-45 days. New construction and complex transactions may take 60-90 days.

What are the most important milestones in a transaction?

Key milestones include: offer acceptance, inspection period completion, appraisal, mortgage approval, final walkthrough, and closing. Missing any of these deadlines can delay or derail the transaction.

How can I help my clients stay on track?

Provide a detailed timeline like this one, set clear expectations, respond promptly to questions, and follow up proactively before each deadline. Communication is key to smooth transactions.

What should I include in transaction milestones?

Include the phase name, target date, responsible party, required documents, and contingency deadlines. Break large tasks into smaller action items so clients know exactly what to expect.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I email new leads?

For new leads, email every 2-3 days for the first 2 weeks. This keeps you top of mind without overwhelming them. After the initial burst, switch to weekly check-ins with valuable content.

Should I include phone calls in my nurture sequence?

Yes! Phone calls convert at much higher rates than email alone. Try to schedule at least one call between days 5-10. Use the "just checking in" angle — agents who make 3+ touches within 30 days close significantly more deals.

How do I know if my nurture sequence is working?

Track these metrics: open rate (aim for 35%+), click rate (aim for 5%+), reply rate (aim for 2%+), and most importantly — appointment scheduled. A/B test subject lines and content types to continuously improve performance.

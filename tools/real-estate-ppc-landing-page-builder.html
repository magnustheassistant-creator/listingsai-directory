Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a real estate PPC landing page convert?

The three keys: (1) A clear, specific headline that matches the ad intent. (2) A valuable, specific offer (not "sign up for our newsletter"). (3) Minimal friction in the form — only ask for name, email, and phone. Remove all navigation and distractions.

How many fields should be in my lead capture form?

Keep it to 3-4 fields maximum: Name, Email, Phone, and one qualification question (like "Are you looking to buy or sell?" or "What's your timeline?"). More fields kill conversion rates. You can qualify leads after.

Should I use a different landing page for each campaign?

Yes! Match your landing page headline to your ad headline as closely as possible. If you're running "First-Time Buyer Guide in Round Rock" ads, the landing page should say "Get Your Free First-Time Buyer Guide for Round Rock" — not a generic page. Specificity = higher conversion rates.

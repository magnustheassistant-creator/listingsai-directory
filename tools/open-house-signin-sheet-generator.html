Frequently Asked Questions

      

        
Can I print this sign-in sheet?

        
Yes! Click the "Print Sheet" button to open your browser's print dialog. The layout is optimized for standard letter-size paper (8.5" x 11").

      

      

        
How do I follow up with these leads effectively?

        
Use a CRM tool to organize your new contacts. ManyChat is excellent for setting up automated text follow-ups that keep you top of mind without being pushy.

      

      

        
What's the best way to qualify open house visitors?

        
Listen more than you talk. Ask open-ended questions like "What are you looking for in a home?" and "What's your timeline?" Then match their needs to what you have available.

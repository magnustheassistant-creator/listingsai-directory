Frequently Asked Questions

What is a buyer lead intake form?

A buyer lead intake form is a questionnaire real estate agents use to collect key information from potential buyers — including budget, property preferences, timeline, and financing — to qualify leads and serve them better.

Why should I use a buyer intake form at open houses?

Open houses generate high-intent leads but most agents lose that data within 24 hours. A structured intake form captures name, contact, budget, and preferences so you can follow up with relevant listings and close more deals.

📋 Letter Writing Tips

📅 Call 3-5 Days After

Send your first letter, wait 3-5 days, then call. They need time to read it before you call. Your voicemail should reference the letter specifically.

✉️ Send 3+ Letters

One letter rarely works. Plan a 3-letter sequence over 2-3 weeks: soft approach first, then more direct, then urgency-based final attempt.

📊 Include Market Data

Expired listings often failed due to pricing. Bring recent comps and neighborhood data — new information is the #1 reason homeowners respond.

🎯 Personalize Everything

Reference the specific address, original price, and days on market. Generic letters go straight to the trash. Make it feel written for them.

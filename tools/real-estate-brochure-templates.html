Brochure Design Best Practices

🖼️ Lead with Emotion

Don't just list features — paint a picture. 'Wake up to panoramic mountain views' beats '6 bedrooms with mountain view.' Buyers buy lifestyle, not square footage.

📐 The 3-Second Rule

Buyers decide whether to read your brochure in 3 seconds. Make the cover image and headline irresistible. Use a bold question or statement: 'What if your morning coffee came with this view?'

🔢 Stats That Sell

Include concrete numbers: 'Built in 2022,' '$4,200/year savings from solar,' '15 min to downtown.' Vague claims ('close to everything') don't convince anyone.

📱 Digital + Print

Generate a QR code linking to a digital version of your brochure with a virtual tour. Tech-savvy buyers expect interactive content. Canva and QR Code Generator offer free QR creation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should be on a real estate brochure?

Essential elements: property address, price, bed/bath/sqft, high-quality photos, a compelling description, agent contact info, and your brokerage branding. QR codes linking to the listing are increasingly popular.

How do I make a real estate brochure?

Use our free AI brochure template generator to create professional copy in seconds. Download the result and pair with your property photos for a polished, print-ready brochure.

What size should a real estate brochure be?

Standard sizes are 8.5x11 (single page) or trifold 11x17 (three panels). The single page format works best for digital sharing; trifold is ideal for print handouts at open houses.

# Free Seller Net Proceeds Calculator — ListingsAI

**Instantly calculate how much you'll actually keep from selling your home.** Accounts for mortgage payoff, agent commissions, closing costs, transfer taxes, and capital gains.

## What This Tool Does

A comprehensive home sale net proceeds calculator that shows sellers exactly how much they'll walk away with after all costs. Includes capital gains tax estimation for primary residences vs. investment properties.

## How to Use

1. Enter the **anticipated sale price** of your home
2. Enter your **remaining mortgage balance**
3. Input your **agent commission rate** (default is 5%)
4. Select your **state** for transfer tax calculation
5. Enter your **purchase price and improvements** for capital gains calculation
6. Click **Calculate Net Proceeds** for instant results

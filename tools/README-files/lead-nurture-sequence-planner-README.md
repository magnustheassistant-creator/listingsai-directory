# Lead Nurture Sequence Planner — ListingsAI

**Plan effective real estate email nurture sequences in minutes — free, no signup required.** Works in any browser.

## What This Tool Does

Maps out complete email nurture sequences for real estate leads at every stage of the funnel. Enter your lead type and funnel stage, and get a complete timeline, content plan, and message strategy for turning leads into clients.

## How to Use

1. Select the **lead type** (buyer, seller, investor, FSBO, expired, sphere)
2. Choose your **nurture stage** (cold, warm, hot, converted)
3. Pick your **content focus** (education, market updates, listings, scripts, tips)
4. Click **Plan Sequence** to generate your complete nurture timeline
5. Follow the day-by-day content plan to move leads through your funnel

## Key Features

- Day-by-day nurture sequence mapped out (14, 21, 30, or 60 day plans)
- Content types: educational, promotional, value-add, urgency
- Email frequency recommendations per stage
- Subject line suggestions for each email
- Call-to-action recommendations per email
- Converts cold leads into appointment-ready prospects

## Use Cases

- Onboarding new leads from your website
- Following up after open houses
- Re-engaging cold sphere of influence contacts
- Nurturing buyers who aren't ready to buy yet
- Keeping in touch with past clients

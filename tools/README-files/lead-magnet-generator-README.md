# Lead Magnet Generator — ListingsAI

**Generate powerful lead magnets for real estate agents — free, no signup required.** Works in any browser.

## What This Tool Does

Creates high-converting lead magnets for real estate professionals: checklists, cheat sheets, market reports, and guides tailored to your target audience. Stop spending money on generic lead magnets — generate ones specifically for your market.

## How to Use

1. Select your **property type** (residential, commercial, land, multi-family)
2. Enter your **agent name** for personalized output
3. Describe your **target audience or situation** (first-time buyers, luxury buyers, sellers, etc.)
4. Click **Generate** to create your lead magnet
5. Copy or download — then use it to capture leads

## Key Features

- Multiple lead magnet formats: checklists, guides, reports, cheat sheets
- Customized for your specific property type and audience
- Print-friendly design for physical handouts
- Digital-ready for email and social media
- Instant download — no email required upfront
- Built-in lead capture strategies

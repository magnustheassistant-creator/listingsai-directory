# FSBO Cold Email Generator — ListingsAI

**Generate effective cold email templates for FSBO outreach — free, no signup required.** Works in any browser.

## What This Tool Does

Creates non-confrontational, value-first cold email sequences specifically designed for reaching FSBO (For Sale By Owner) homeowners. FSBOs are motivated sellers — this tool helps you contact them without feeling like a typical real estate agent pitch.

## How to Use

1. Select the **FSBO type** you're targeting (traditional FSBO, expired listing converted, reducer)
2. Choose your **approach style** (educational, market data, appointment, hybrid)
3. Enter **property details** or any notes about the situation
4. Click **Generate Emails** for your complete FSBO outreach sequence
5. Copy individual emails or download the full sequence

## Key Features

- 5-7 email sequence with proven FSBO outreach scripts
- Subject lines that get opens (curiosity, value, market data angles)
- Non-pushy approach that respects FSBOs' desire to sell solo
- Timing recommendations optimized for FSBO response
- Follow-up sequence for unanswered emails
- Mobile-friendly and works in any browser

## Use Cases

- Farming neighborhoods with high FSBO concentration
- Following up on FSBO door knocking
- Converting FSBOs who haven't sold yet
- Building relationships with FSBOs before they convert

# Real Estate Email Sequence Builder

Build complete, automated email sequences for any real estate campaign in minutes. Choose from FSBO, Lead Nurture, Sphere of Influence, Expired Listing, Just Listed, or Farm campaigns. Each sequence includes 5-6 done-for-you emails with subject lines, body copy, and CTAs ready to import into your email platform.

## Features

- 6 campaign types: FSBO, Lead Nurture, Sphere of Influence, Expired Listing, Just Listed, Farm
- 5-6 emails per sequence with subject lines, body copy, and CTAs
- Customizable agent name, target address, price range, UVP, contact info
- Copy all emails to clipboard
- Download as TXT file
- FAQ schema for SEO
- Mobile responsive

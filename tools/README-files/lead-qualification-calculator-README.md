# Lead Qualification Calculator — ListingsAI

**Calculate and score real estate leads with a free lead qualification tool — no signup required.** Works in any browser.

## What This Tool Does

Scores your real estate leads using proven qualification criteria (Budget, Motivation, Timeline, Authority, Fit). Enter lead details and get an immediate readiness score with specific talking points to advance the conversation.

## How to Use

1. Enter your **lead's basic info** (name, contact, property interest)
2. Input their **budget range** and **financing status**
3. Select their **motivation level** (just browsing → actively must sell)
4. Choose their **timeline** (6+ months → under 30 days)
5. Click **Calculate Score** for instant lead qualification results
6. Get specific talking points to move the lead forward

## Key Features

- 100-point lead scoring system with 5 key criteria
- Instant readiness rating: Cold / Warm / Hot / Converted
- Specific talking points for each score range
- Buyer vs seller qualification criteria
- Mobile-friendly assessment form
- Results in seconds — no data stored

## Use Cases

- Triage inbound leads by priority
- Prepare for listing appointments with seller leads
- Qualify buyer leads before showing properties
- Focus your time on the highest-converting prospects
- Train new agents on lead qualification

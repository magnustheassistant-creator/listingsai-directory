## What This Tool Does

The Real Estate Investment Calculator analyzes deals with IRR, cap rate, cash-on-cash return, and 5-year projections. Free tool for investors evaluating rental properties, fix-and-flip, and commercial real estate.

## Features

- **Full Deal Analysis** — Purchase price, down payment, loan terms
- **Income & Expenses** — Rent, taxes, insurance, HOA, vacancy, management
- **Key Metrics Dashboard** — Cash-on-cash, cap rate, IRR, total profit
- **5-Year Projection** — Appreciation, equity buildup, cash flow forecast
- **Exit Analysis** — Net sale proceeds after selling costs and loan payoff
- **Copy Report** — Export full analysis to clipboard

# Cold Email Sequence Generator — ListingsAI

**Generate multi-step cold email sequences for real estate prospecting — free, no signup required.** Works in any browser.

## What This Tool Does

Creates complete 5-7 step cold email campaigns for real estate lead generation. Enter your target market and property type, and get subject lines, email body copy, timing intervals, and follow-up sequences ready to use.

## How to Use

1. Select your **target audience** (buyers, sellers, investors, FSBO, expired)
2. Choose your **property type** (residential, commercial, land, multi-family)
3. Enter any **specific details** about your market or situation
4. Click **Generate Sequence** to get your complete campaign
5. Copy individual emails or download the full sequence

## Key Features

- 5-7 email sequence with subject lines per email
- Timing intervals between emails (day 1, day 3, day 7, etc.)
- Scripts proven in real estate prospecting
- Customizable for any market and property type
- Copy to clipboard or download as text file
- Mobile-friendly — works on any device

## Use Cases

- Prospecting expired listing owners
- Reaching FSBOs with a non-confrontational approach
- Following up after open houses
- Farming a new neighborhood
- Building your sphere of influence

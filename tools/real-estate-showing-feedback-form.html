How to Get Better Showing Feedback

⏱️ Send Within 30 Minutes

Feedback requests sent within 30 minutes of a showing have 3x higher response rates than those sent hours later. Use ManyChat or a CRM to auto-send the moment the showing is logged as complete. The buyer's impression is freshest right after the showing.

📱 Use Text, Not Email

Text messages have a 98% open rate vs 20% for email. Send the feedback form via text with a direct link. Keep the message short: 'Thanks for seeing 1842 Oakridge! Quick 2-min feedback: [link] — your input helps the seller and helps me find you the right home faster.'

🎯 Ask About Price Perception

The single most important question: 'Does this home feel like a good value at the asking price?' Yes/No/Not Sure tells you everything. If buyers say no to value but yes to everything else, the price needs to drop. If they say yes to value but still won't offer, it's a conditions or competition issue.

📊 Compile Weekly for Sellers

Don't share feedback piecemeal — give sellers a weekly summary: X showings, average rating Y, top 3 buyer comments, top 3 concerns, recommended actions. This turns you into an indispensable market advisor and justifies any price adjustments you recommend.

Frequently Asked Questions

    

      
How do I make a real estate flyer for free?

      
Simply fill in the property details above — address, price, bedrooms, bathrooms, and description — choose a color theme, and click Generate. Your flyer is ready to copy as HTML and use anywhere. No signup required.

    

    

      
Can I use these flyers for commercial real estate listings?

      
Yes. The flyer HTML can be customized for commercial properties. Use the description field to highlight square footage, zoning, CapRate, or investment potential. For commercial flyers, add your broker information in the contact section.

    

    

      
Are the flyer templates printable?

      
Yes. The generated HTML is designed for standard US Letter (8.5 x 11 inch) printing. Copy the HTML into any word processor (Word, Google Docs) or print directly from your browser. Use "Print to PDF" for digital distribution.

    

    

      
Do I need design skills to use this flyer maker?

      
No design skills required. Just enter your property details and the tool generates a professional flyer layout automatically. Every flyer looks polished with consistent typography, spacing, and color themes.

    

    

      
Can I add my own photo to the flyer?

      
The HTML output is editable. Replace the placeholder image area with an img tag pointing to your property photo URL, or paste the HTML into a tool like Canva to add images before printing.

Pro Tips for High-Converting Flyers

📸 Lead with a Great Photo

The cover photo is the first thing buyers notice. Use the best exterior or interior shot. Avoid cluttered rooms, bad lighting, and personal items visible.

💲 Price Anchoring Works

Always include the price prominently. Buyers are scanning for value. If you've recently reduced price, show the original price crossed out.

🎯 Less Text, More Scannable

Buyers spend an average of 5-8 seconds on a flyer. Use bullet points, short sentences, and big numbers. Save the full description for the MLS listing.

📱 Include a QR Code

Generate a free QR code linking to your listing or virtual tour. Place it at the bottom of the flyer so mobile users can instantly view more details.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a real estate flyer effective?

Great real estate flyers have a professional design, clear headline, key property facts (beds/baths/sqft/price), 2-3 high-quality photos, and your contact information. Less text is more — buyers should want to learn more and call you.

How do I make a real estate flyer for free?

Use our free AI flyer maker to generate the property description and headline copy in seconds. Pair the AI-generated text with your property photos and broker branding for a professional result.

Should real estate flyers be digital or print?

Both! Digital flyers work great for email, social media, and text messages. Print flyers are still expected at open houses and as leave-behinds. Always have digital versions ready to send instantly.

Pro Tips for Door Knocking Success

🎯 The 3-Second Rule

You have 3 seconds to make a good impression before they decide to engage or close the door. Smile, make eye contact, and lead with value.

📋 Always Leave Something Behind

Business cards are minimum. Better: a market report, farm letter, or property flyer. Give them something to remember you by.

🔁 Follow Up Within 48 Hours

Send a text or email within 2 days referencing your door knock. "Great meeting you yesterday — here is that market report I promised."

⏰ Best Days: Sat–Sun Morning

Saturday 9am–12pm is prime time. Sunday afternoon works too. Avoid weekdays unless evening (5–7pm).

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I say when door knocking for real estate?

Start with a genuine compliment about the property, introduce yourself, offer a specific helpful piece of information (market update, recently sold home in the area), then ask a non-pressuring question like 'Have you ever thought about what your home might be worth today?'

Is door knocking still effective for real estate agents?

Yes — it's one of the most effective lead generation activities for buyer and seller leads, despite being uncomfortable. FSBOs and expired listings are particularly receptive. The key is consistency and a genuine, helpful approach.

How many doors should I knock per day?

Most productive agents aim for 50-100 doors per day during dedicated prospecting time. Quality matters more than quantity — track your conversations and follow up on every opportunity to build relationships.

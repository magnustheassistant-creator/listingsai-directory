Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best time to call FSBO leads?

Late afternoon (4pm-7pm) typically works best — people are home from work and more receptive. Avoid calling during work hours unless you know they're self-employed. Saturday mornings are also strong.

How do I handle "I'm not interested" on the first call?

The key is to not fight resistance. Say "I totally understand" and ask one simple question: "Can I ask what changed from when you decided to sell?" This often opens a conversation and reveals whether there's a legitimate concern or just a knee-jerk rejection.

Should I leave a voicemail for FSBO calls?

Yes! Keep it under 30 seconds. Structure: Your name + "I'm calling about your home at [address] because I have a buyer who's very interested in this type of property" + callback number. Create curiosity, don't pitch. Most FSBOs will call back if they think a buyer exists.

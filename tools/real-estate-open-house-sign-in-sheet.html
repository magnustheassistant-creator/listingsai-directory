Frequently Asked Questions

Why is an open house sign-in sheet important?
An open house sign-in sheet captures lead contact information at your most important real estate marketing events. On average, real estate agents get 1-5 sign-ins per open house, and each lead represents a potential buyer or seller. Without a sign-in sheet, you have no way to follow up with interested visitors — your biggest missed opportunity at every open house.
What information should I collect at an open house?
Collect at minimum: full name, email address, and phone number. Also ask: current address, price range, timeline, pre-approval status, and how they heard about the open house. This data helps you qualify leads and tailor your follow-up. Our sign-in sheet includes all key fields plus notes for personalization.
How do I follow up with open house leads effectively?
Follow up within 24 hours of the open house — the longer you wait, the less likely they will respond. Send a personalized email referencing the property they toured, include 2-3 similar listings, and ask if they'd like a private showing. Our Buyer Lead Intake Form tool helps you capture and organize these leads for CRM entry.
